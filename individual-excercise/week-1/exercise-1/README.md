## Overview of the results

This dataset is used to investigate how the perceived credibility of influencers affects purchase intention and actual purchasing behaviour among Indonesian Generation Z consumers in a social commerce context. A three-month behavioural observation study involving 500 Indonesian Generation Z consumers aged between 18 and 26 from various major cities.
